## Verification & Recommendation (added after cross-checks)

**The resampled values are verified correct; the stored values carry a
systematic dark bias.**

1. **Mechanism found:** each swatch photo is a 3×3 grid of raised paint tiles
   with dark recessed grooves. The image's geometric CENTRE is a groove
   intersection — the original centre-point extraction sampled recessed
   shadow. The region median lands on tile surface.
2. **Page-render cross-check:** sampling through the PDF's own rendering
   pipeline agrees with the raw-image medians to ±2 RGB units on every
   tested paint — the resampler is internally consistent.
3. **Visual check** (`swatch_resample_check.png`): Leadbelcher, Dark Sea Grey
   and Pallid Wych Flesh swatches beside stored vs resampled colours — the
   stored values are visibly too dark in every case; Leadbelcher was stored
   as near-black (#383330) but is a mid silver (#6D6B69).
4. **Metallics were hit hardest** (a centre point on a sparkle field is a
   lottery draw): ~21% of matched metallics drifted > ΔE00 3, vs ~4% of
   mattes. Median internal spread: metallic 9.3 vs matte 3.4 Lab units.
   This also explains the production "black armour → Leadbelcher"
   confusions: the silvers were stored as near-blacks.
5. **Coverage after the second check (user query re: 19 unmatched):**
   17 of the 19 were REAL paints recovered by fixing three label-parsing
   defects (multi-line labels starting fractionally above the swatch edge;
   long labels bleeding under neighbouring swatches; Vallejo VMC/VGC/VMetC
   name collisions). Final state: **1,216 / 1,312 matched; every opaque
   paint in the DB has a swatch sample; the 96 without one are exactly the
   washes/inks/shades** (transparent media, absent from the chart, values
   remain assumed). 2 residual 'WPF' label fragments are layout debris.

**Recommended patch policy (for your sign-off):**
- Adopt the resampled region-median lab/hex for ALL 1,216 matched paints
  (uniform provenance — not just the drifted ones), storing per-swatch
  `swatch_spread` as a reliability field.
- Keep stored values for the 96 washes/inks/shades (flag `color_source:
  'assumed'` vs `'swatch-median'`).
- Then regenerate anchors, recipe edges, golden fixtures and the frontend DB,
  and re-run the full suite + harness before deploy.
